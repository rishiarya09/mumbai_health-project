Har Gully Healthy - A Dream. . . .

Mumbai is a hub of dreams and hopes. One of the many industries that thrive in the Maximum City, healthcare wasn't the most popular one until the pandemic slowed us down.

It was looming in our eyes more than ever that a single city regularly becomes a guiding lamp for neighbouring states and people who travel from as far as 2000 kms to seek quality healthcare.

There is a stark disparity in the distribution of healthcare services and model of delivery.

Mumbai Health Project aims to create a conduit between the extremes to arrive at a balance, providing better access to quality healthcare to the section of the society that struggles the most both financially and in terms of lack of awareness.

A step closer to universal healthcare, come join us in this journey of making

Har Gully, A Healthy Gully!





Mission

  • ☛ Affordable Healthcare within reach initiate.
  • ☛ Preventive Healthcare Behavioural Change amongst the Urban Poor with a Focus on Infectious Diseases.
  • ☛ Provide a Platform for Doctors to create a Socially Motivated Entrepreneurial Community.

Vision

Take Healthcare To Every Gully
