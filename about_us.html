About Us

WHO WE ARE

A small team of healthcare professionals who are attached to the city and tied together with a common goal : Take the healthcare professionals to every gully of the city

OUR MISSION

To revisit the "family doctor" concept and present it as an enabler to the government's efforts to improve healthcare services with a focus on low income inhabitations

OUR VISION

Universal healthcare becomes a dream within reach alongside a strong arm of behavioural change towards community health.
